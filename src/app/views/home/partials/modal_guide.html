Welcome to Blockr!

Beta Notice

While using Blockr keep in mind that it is in Beta. Which means that things may not work the way they were intended, and things are always changing. Future releases of Blockr may not support projects that you create now, so take that into account while making them.

Follow the Development

Want to see what's coming next, or just curious and want to follow the development. Click on the trello icon (the third icon) in the top right.

Feedback

Found a bug? Something not working the way you thought? Have an amazing idea? Click on the chat bubble icon in the top right.
